## Problem Statement

Modern investors spread their assets across multiple platforms — stocks on Robinhood or Fidelity, crypto on Coinbase or Binance, ETFs on Schwab — but no single tool shows the complete picture in one place. Constantly switching between apps makes it nearly impossible to understand your true net worth, track overall performance, or make informed investment decisions. Wealthview solves this by securely connecting all your exchanges and brokerages in one dashboard, giving you a unified real-time view of your entire portfolio alongside AI-powered insights tailored to your investment strategy.
